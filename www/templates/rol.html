(Rainbow Of Love)

Love comes in different colours.. Like the bright rainbow in the skies.. From tempting momentary sparks.. To everlasting blue flames!

Yeah; it is a spectrum.. Flirtatious alluring looks.. Generating twinkling sparks.. That may dwindle or even fade!

Be careful, be cautious my dear.. Yeah; it’s very easy to bury love.. Even when it is not dead.. Because the sparks are gone!

Hmm.. You get to know each other.. Strong points and faults emerge.. Quarrels, fights, and squabbles may come.. You make up, or some separate over time!

There are no raindrops alike.. Even identical twins differ in their perceptions.. And inclinations to life!

The major global problem.. In the love department.. Realistically right now... Is that many live just for sparks!

However; sparks are sparks.. Especially if they remain so.. Sparks cannot boil water.. Except they generate flames; they die!

Flames give heat.. Love’s culinary catalyst. Mankind has evolved above the beasts.. Most delicious meals need to be cooked!

Precious jewel..This day.. I wish you the brightest light.. Longer lasting than temporary sparks.. So fulfilling.. So calm.. So cool..

Warm, delicately serene. Yet depicting great Joy.. Diamond of inestimable value.. I have a fervent wish for you!

Yes; Powerful and deep.. Beyond the glitters of Gliterratti.. In all spheres of your life.. As lovely as you are!

Yeah you deserve it.. Long lasting.. Soothing.. Indeed; my fervent wish for you.. Is the BLUE FLAME!
