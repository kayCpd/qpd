(Stegomyia Mosquito)

Wobbly legs.. Unstable features.. Pointed mouth and snorting through the nostrils!

Your unsuspecting victim, is naked.. He lays bare, Waiting for you to feast!

You deep your fangs.. Your penetrating proboscis.. Into his flesh.. Yes you feed from his blood!

Initially it is ticklish.. A bit dazed he becomes.. Like the tipsy feeling.. After taking some wine!

As the dizziness deepens.. He realises.. To his utter chagrin.. That his live is ebbing away!

So long so far.. The journey of his life, is closing up.. What a shame!

Dust to dust.. Ashes to ashes.. And so the victim.. Crosses to the other side!
