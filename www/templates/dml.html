(Darting Moves Of Love)

Starring starry Light.. Like the wind that blows far away.. Like the storms you cannot catch.. Like the tornado that passed us by!

O what a tight night? Nighties that are so nice.. Negligees that won’t neglect.. Needling Stallions of Love!

Loving me till eternity.. Tempting thoughts of tall trees.. Everlasting green trees.. Telling stories with the wind!

Unseen literary pens.. Moving through the landscape of time.. Inscribing intensely deep romantic verses.. Which only cupid can painstakingly decipher!

Pumping pleasure, prompting.. Pompous moves, unlike the Pope’s.. Popping eyes that probe.. With lofty lustful looks!

Alas how should we roll? Rollicking frolics of desire.. Darting moves of Love.. As we dance through the night!
