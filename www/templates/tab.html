(The Alabaster Box)

She is not opening.. A Mandarin Pandora’s Box.. No tricks from Honduras.. No games from Panama! It’s just the depth.. of her unprecedented inner feeling. An astounding Intense affection.. towards the man of his dreams!

She is sophisticated but not truncated by her education and impressive intelligence.. Yea; she got struck by Cupid’s arrow and overwhelmed by passion!

She stood up against all odds to gain his attention, to win his love; She struck everyone like a thunderbolt!

They thought she couldn’t cook, they thought she couldn’t wash, she surprised all by excelling in everything she ironed and kept all impeccably clean!

Her manners are legendary, her patience is surprising, she is fully loaded with goodness.. A priceless Alabaster box!

O sweet bride; cupid’s lass.. Beautiful to behold an epitome of perfection. Prince Charming has been knocked out for sixpence and begins to dote over her!
