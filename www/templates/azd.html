(Azondikhulula)

Azondikhulula..indeed by jove.. Yes come and rescue him.. Yeah; come and set him free.. Push has really come to shove!

He has lost count of the days.. The hours have become irrelevant.. Irreplaceable are the deep feelings in his phakhati; His deepest inner being!

Someone said; Why should he? Another said; How could he? O tighten the bolts of the door.. Guard his heart from love!

Then Princess Charming said to him: You are...my Diamond Marinated in liquid Gold, solidified with Rubies!

Sculptured in Beauty.. Perfected in Elegance.. Etched therein.. Is your pet name!
